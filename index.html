Welcome to Mind Matters

Mental health is just as important as physical health. This website is dedicated to raising awareness and providing simple tools to support your mental wellbeing.

"You don't have to be positive all the time. It's perfectly okay to feel sad, angry, annoyed, frustrated, scared, or anxious. Having feelings doesn't make you a negative person. It makes you human." — Lori Deschene

When feeling anxious, try this simple breathing technique:

Breathe in as the circle grows, breathe out as it shrinks. Repeat for 1 minute.

5. Today's Mental Health Tip

Take a 5-minute break to step outside and notice 3 things you can see, 2 things you can hear, and 1 thing you can feel. This simple mindfulness exercise can help ground you in the present moment.
